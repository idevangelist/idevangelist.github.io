The life and times of a developer evangelist can be fun, enlightening and highly intense. This website will hold a collection of content that guides you through that role and helps you understand if its for you.

This site will also contain lots of tips and suggestions for those currently in this role, gathered from a range of experiences of Developer Evangelists across the world.
